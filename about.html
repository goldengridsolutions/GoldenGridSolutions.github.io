What We Do

We reduce energy consumption.
Then we prove it.

Most energy efficiency solutions rely on projections. We don't. Every enPact deployment begins with a verified baseline and ends with continuous monitoring that confirms savings are real, persistent, and exactly what was promised.

We work with energy-intensive commercial and industrial facilities — food production, cold storage, hospitality, healthcare, manufacturing, and more — where the energy bill is large enough that a 10–25% reduction makes a material difference to the bottom line.

Our role is to identify where the opportunity sits in your facility, match the right technology to the right load, manage the deployment, and ensure you have the verified performance data you need for internal reporting and ESG disclosure.

Verified results — not projections
Every result on this site comes from a real deployment, measured using IPMVP-aligned methodology with controlled before-and-after comparison periods. No modeled estimates. No assumed baselines.
Performance-backed by Emissis
Energy savings are backed by a performance guarantee issued by Emissis. The technology either delivers or Emissis stands behind it. That accountability starts before installation and continues through ongoing monitoring.
Retrofit — no equipment replacement
