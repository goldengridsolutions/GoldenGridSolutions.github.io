The Problem

High loads. Mixed systems.
No room to shut down.

Pubs, bars, and restaurants run a dense mix of refrigeration, HVAC, kitchen equipment, and lighting — all simultaneously, all day. Most of that energy is wasted before it reaches the end load.

🍺
Bar refrigeration runs around the clock
Cellar coolers, back-bar fridges, display units, and draught beer systems cycle continuously — even during closed hours. Compressors work harder than needed on every cycle, accumulating significant waste across a full estate.
⚡
Kitchen and HVAC loads add electrical noise
Commercial kitchen equipment, extraction systems, and air handling units create voltage fluctuations and harmonic distortion across every circuit in the venue — wasting energy invisibly upstream of all your equipment.
🏢
Multi-site operators face portfolio-wide costs
For pub and restaurant groups, energy is one of the largest controllable overheads. A consistent, repeatable saving per site compounds into a material estate-wide financial and carbon benefit — but it has to work across mixed site profiles to be deployable at scale.
The Solution

Installed in hours.
Savings from day one.

Both enPact technologies retrofit to existing systems without modification. No shutdown, no disruption to service, no impact on food safety or guest comfort. Each targets a different energy loss mechanism.

Optimizes power quality across every circuit simultaneously — bar refrigeration motors, extraction fans, kitchen equipment, HVAC, and lighting. Connects to your incoming supply in parallel. Retrofit installation — no changes to your existing equipment or systems.
